Privacy Policy

Learn how Closana collects, uses, and protects your information.

Privacy Policy

Closana will not rent or sell potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information to anyone.

Closana OÜ (“Closana”) may operate on other websites. It is Closana’s policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect while operating our websites.

Website Visitors

Like most website operators, Closana collects non-personally-identifying information such as browser type, language preference, referring site, and the time of each visitor request. This helps us understand how users interact with our site. Occasionally, we may release non-personal data in aggregate form, such as usage trends.

Closana may also collect potentially personally-identifying information such as IP addresses for logged-in users. These are disclosed only under specific circumstances, such as blog comment visibility for site admins.

Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information

Some interactions with Closana require us to collect personal information. For example, users making transactions may need to provide personal and financial details. This data is collected only when necessary and is not disclosed beyond what’s described in this policy.

Aggregated Statistics

Closana may collect data on visitor behavior in order to improve our services. This information is aggregated and anonymized before being shared publicly or with third parties.

Protection of Personally-Identifying Information

Closana only shares personal data with employees, contractors, and affiliates who need it for processing and have agreed not to disclose it. Some of them may operate outside your home country, and by using Closana’s services, you consent to this transfer. Closana will not sell or rent this information to anyone.

Closana may disclose personal data only when legally required or to protect the rights and safety of Closana, users, or the public.

Cookies

Closana uses cookies to track user preferences and activity. Users can set their browser to refuse cookies, though this may affect functionality.

Business Transfers

In the event of a business sale or bankruptcy, user data may be transferred to the new owner as part of the business assets.

Ads

Ads may be served by third-party partners using cookies to deliver relevant advertisements. This policy does not govern third-party cookie usage.

Privacy Policy Changes

Closana may occasionally update this policy. We encourage users to review it regularly. Continued use of the site constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.
