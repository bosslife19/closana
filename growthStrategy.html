What is strategic consulting?

Growth strategy consulting is a service that helps companies find and choose the best way to grow, scale, and earn more money. It’s like bringing in a team of experts who add strategic value by advising on how to improve your growth operations, business unit and marketing strategies.

What exactly do business growth experts examine?

In general, the ultimate goal of growth strategy consulting is simple—help your business grow faster and smarter and simultaneously avoid mistakes that can slow it down.

Target opportunities: A growth consultant leverages predictive modeling and data analytics to identify the most promising market segments for your business. Professionals in growth strategy consulting typically analyze customer demographics, competitive landscape, and market trends to craft the best startup growth strategy.

Failures: Even the best business strategy consulting won’t work if you don’t analyze your failures. Only when you know the root causes of operational breakdowns and are able to fix them, you’ll have room for further growth. A strategic business consultant knows this very well; that’s why they carefully examine all your business processes, especially the ones that are failing.

Competitors: There’s always competition, and growth strategy consulting accounts for it. SWOT analysis, market research, and benchmarking against competitors—all this helps ensure the best product strategy and consulting.

Opportunities for mergers and acquisitions: Growth hacking agencies provide thorough due diligence to find the right M&A options. They want to make sure that all is okay with your finances, the risks are minimized, and the cultural fit aligns with your business goals before moving forward with any deals.

How to check if a growth hacking consultant really helps your business?

If you want to measure the effectiveness of growth strategy consulting, try to track the following business metrics and KPIs:

  • Revenue growth;
  • Customer acquisition cost (CAC);
  • Lead conversion rates;
  • Operational efficiency;
  • Customer lifetime value (CLTV).

If you see these KPIs improve over time, the suggested business strategy by a consultant works.

Who should hire a business strategy advisor?

Early-stage startups

If you’re a first-time founder, you might get lost in the business jungles. How to get the initial traction? Where to get funding? How to achieve a product-market fit? How to gain brand recognition? Small business strategy consultants can help you solve all these questions.

Growing businesses

Many companies at this stage need strategic business consulting. Your sales may grow so fast that you start having problems managing high demand, supply chains, and product lines. A growth hacking consultant can help you handle this rapid growth and build a basis for your expansion.

Mature companies

If you think mature companies don’t need growth strategy consulting, that’s where you go wrong. They need help, no doubt they do! However, their problems are more complex and require rock-solid business growth strategies. Growth stagnation is the most common issue here.

FAQ

What is strategy consulting, and who is a business strategist?

Growth strategy consulting is a service that helps companies find and choose the best way to grow, scale, and earn more money. A business strategist is a professional who analyzes your company, market, and competitors to advise you on how to make smarter decisions, tackle issues, and hit all your goals.

Which areas of my business would a growth hacking consultant analyze first?

A growth hacking consultant will first analyze your business model, market positioning, customer acquisition processes, and operational efficiency. They will also deep dive into your sales funnels, marketing strategies, and financial performance. This will help them see what’s working well and what needs change.

How can growth hacking consultants help me?

If you start thinking about growth strategy consulting, first of all, look at the expert’s experience, expertise, and track record. A good business strategy professional knows your industry, market, and which KPIs to focus on. Also, look at their analytical skills, creativity, and ability to handle data and find common ground with your team.

How to choose the right growth hacking consultant?

If you want to get the best growth strategy consulting services, look at the following aspects when choosing a consultant:

1. Ask for a track record of success. Look at how they helped other businesses—eye increased revenue, traffic, or conversions.

2. Choose a consultant who knows your industry and market. The matter is that growth strategy consulting tactics that work in one industry may not work in another; that’s why choose a professional with relevant experience.

3. A good consultant knows which KPIs align with your business goals and focuses on them.

4. They teach your team how to implement a strategy so that it can be replicated even after they finish working with your company.

5. A growth hacking consultant must be collaborative. In case the atmosphere in the team is competitive or even hostile, the successful implementation of a growth strategy might be in danger.

6. They must have analytical skills and know how to handle data because growth hacking is all about testing and optimizing. Thus, make sure you’re searching for someone who relies on data when making decisions and can measure what really works.

7. A growth hacking consultant must be creative and able to think outside the box.

8. Don’t forget to ask for feedback from past clients. In such a way, you will get a sense of their work style and the level of professionalism. This will help you understand whether such business growth consultancy suits you, your business needs, and your goals.

When does hiring an external growth strategy consultant make sense?

A lack of experience and internal resources can lead to the failure of your startup. A successful start is only the beginning, then comes scaling – a risky stage with plenty of pitfalls that founders are often unaware of. Hiring an external growth strategy consulting firm can help you avoid them, greatly reducing the risk of failure.

How long does it take to see results after growth strategy consulting?

On average, you can see the first results of implemented changes within three to six months. However, major growth and scaling efforts could take 12 months or even longer. Everything depends on your business size and the strategy being implemented.
