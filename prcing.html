FAQ

Will I be charged pre or post-work?

Our billing process is designed to foster a trustful and balanced relationship. Generally, we require a 25% prepayment to kickstart the project. An additional 50% is charged upon completion of a full draft, and the remaining 25% is due prior to the delivery of the editable documents.

Do you charge a success fee based on a successful raise or only flat fee?

Our pricing model is a flat fee structure. We believe this model is in the best interest of our clients and their potential investors. It ensures predictability and fairness, avoiding any potential ambiguities or surprises associated with success fees, which can sometimes be excessive or lead to complications down the line.

Do you provide a refund if I’m not satisfied with the quality of work?

While we don't offer refunds, we operate on a milestone-based work structure. If at any point you decide to halt the project, you're only liable for the work completed up to that point. It's important to us that our clients are fully satisfied – should the work not meet your standards, we pledge to revise and refine until it fulfills your expectations.

Are taxes included in the price?

Absolutely, all our quoted prices include the necessary taxes. However, please be aware that certain bank transfer fees might apply depending on your banking institution's policies. These additional costs, if any, would be the responsibility of the client.

Can I pay in crypto?

Absolutely, we recognize the growing importance of digital currencies and are happy to accept crypto payments alongside more traditional payment methods.
